# Business Impact

## Maintenance value

Remaining useful life prediction supports predictive maintenance by helping organizations plan maintenance before failure occurs.

## Operational benefits

Accurate RUL prediction can help organizations:

- Reduce unscheduled shutdowns
- Improve maintenance planning
- Increase equipment availability
- Reduce operational risk
- Improve spare-parts and labor planning
- Support asset reliability decisions

## Strategic use cases

This approach is relevant to:

- Electric vehicle components
- Batteries and capacitors
- Manufacturing assets
- Renewable energy systems
- Aerospace systems
- High-reliability industrial equipment

## Decision-support value

The model converts sensor history into a remaining-life estimate. This makes it useful for maintenance dashboards, asset health monitoring, and reliability-centered maintenance programs.
